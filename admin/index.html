Sample Work

How I approach real tasks

These examples demonstrate my typical workflows — the same systems and processes I apply when working with actual clients.

Workflow Sample 01
Prior Authorization Request
1
Receive request via fax/email from provider office or pharmacy.
2
Verify patient eligibility and prescription/procedure coverage in the EHR system.
3
Review payer-specific guidelines and gather required clinical documentation.
4
Submit auth request through payer portal or assigned system; log in EMR.
5
Follow up with payer on pending or denied requests; notify provider of outcome.
6
Update patient record with approval details, dates, and authorization number.
Workflow Sample 02
Patient Scheduling & Follow-Up
1
Receive appointment request via phone, email, or online form.
2
Confirm patient's insurance, provider preference, and urgency level.
3
Check provider availability in the scheduling system and book the appointment.
4
Send confirmation with date, time, location, and preparation instructions.
5
Trigger reminder 48 hours prior via phone or automated messaging platform.
6
Document all interactions and update status in the EMR system accordingly.
Workflow Sample 03
Inbox & Task Management System
1
Sort incoming emails and faxes into categories: Urgent, Action Required, FYI, Archive.
2
Flag and escalate time-sensitive items (auth deadlines, patient callbacks) immediately.
3
Process routine requests (eligibility checks, record updates) in batches for efficiency.
4
Log all completed tasks in the shared task tracker with status and timestamps.
5
Send end-of-day summary to supervisor: tasks completed, pending items, blockers.
6
Maintain zero-inbox standard — all items resolved or escalated before close of day.
